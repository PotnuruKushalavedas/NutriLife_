🌱 Understanding Nutrition

About Nutrition

Nutrition is the science of how food nourishes the body — supplying energy, supporting growth, strengthening immunity, and reducing chronic disease risk across every stage of life.

Why Nutrition Matters

Every cell in the human body depends on nutrients obtained from food. Macronutrients (carbohydrates, proteins, and fats) provide energy and structural building blocks, while micronutrients (vitamins and minerals) regulate metabolism, immunity, and organ function.

Understanding age-specific requirements helps families, schools, and healthcare workers deliver targeted dietary guidance — the core mission of this Community Service Project.

  • Supports physical growth and cognitive development
  • Prevents malnutrition and micronutrient deficiencies
  • Reduces risk of diabetes, heart disease, and anemia
  • Promotes healthy aging and quality of life
🍞

Macronutrients

Carbohydrates, proteins, and fats provide energy, support growth, repair tissues, and maintain overall body functions.

💊

Micronutrients

Vitamins and minerals support immunity, bone health, metabolism, oxygen transport, and many vital body processes.

💧

Hydration

Water is essential for digestion, temperature regulation, nutrient transport, and maintaining healthy skin and joints.

⚖️

Dietary Balance

Combining diverse food groups in appropriate portions ensures no single nutrient is over- or under-consumed.

🛡️

Immunity Support

Essential vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants strengthen the immune system, helping the body fight infections and recover faster.

🦴

Bone Health

Calcium, Vitamin D, Magnesium, and Phosphorus strengthen bones and teeth, reducing the risk of fractures and bone loss.

🧠

Brain Health

Essential nutrients support brain growth, memory, learning, and concentration throughout life.

⚡

Energy Production

Carbohydrates, proteins, and healthy fats provide the energy needed for daily activities, growth, learning, and physical performance.

🏃

Growth & Tissue Repair

Proteins, vitamins, and minerals help build muscles, repair tissues, and support healthy growth.

🥗

Food Diversity

Eating a variety of fruits, vegetables, grains, legumes, dairy, and proteins ensures a balanced intake of essential nutrients.
